JAIS FLIGHT

We don't just believe, we know you can fly! Jais Flight takes pride in being the world's longest zipline, stretching 2.83 kilometres at a height of 1,680 metres above sea level across Jebel Jais' dramatic canyons where zipliners reach speeds of up to 160 km/h. Jais Flight embodies the perfect trifecta of your need for speed, height, and distance. Having taken over 120,000 thrill-seekers into "flight" from around the world since its launch in 2018, this high-profile attraction does not disappoint.

JAIS SKY TOUR

Experience the excitement of seeing the Hajar mountain range from a bird's eye view on the region's longest zipline tour. Cover a total of five kilometres at a breathtaking 1,600 metres above sea level, gliding at speeds up to 60 km/h from platform to platform on a series of six ziplines, connected by an impressive 1,250-metre-tall sky bridge. If you are looking to prolong the adrenaline rush after the Jais Flight or simply a more scenic experience, this family-friendly adventure activity is for you! It's a two-hour aerial sightseeing tour with a serious kick.

79.00 AED

Bear Grylls Explorers Camp

Learn dynamic, real-life survival skills in the heart of nature with our professional guides at the first branded camp accommodation in the world from the survival expert, Bear Grylls. Gain confidence outdoors, whether you're here for a half-day course, booked in for an adventure activity, an "exploriential" kid's program or an overnight Primal Survival Course, going into the wild at Jais is the purest state of adventure, perfect for children over 8, families, and team building.
