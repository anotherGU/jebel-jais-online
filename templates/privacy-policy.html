Welcome to Jebel Jais Ras Al Khaimah. We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website and use our services.

Why We Value Your Privacy

Information We Collect

Personal Information

We collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when you:

Name, email address, phone number, payment information, booking details, and any other information you choose to provide.
